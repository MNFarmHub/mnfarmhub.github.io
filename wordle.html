How to Play Wordle

Use !wordle + (5 letter word) to guess a word. Light grey letters have not been guessed. Darn Grey letters are not in the puzzle. Green letters mean the letter is in the puzzle and in the corrrect spot. Yellow letters mean the letter is in the puzzle but in the wrong spot.
